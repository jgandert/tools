Eriksen Flanker Task

Identify the direction of the central target arrow or letter as fast as possible. Ignore the flanking distractors on either side.

How to Play & Examples

A fixation cross (+) will appear, followed briefly by 5 or 7 letters or arrows. Focus only on the middle character.

Key Mappings:

  • Left Target: Press A or Left Arrow (←) if the center target is X, C, S, or <.
  • Right Target: Press L or Right Arrow (→) if the center target is V, B, H, K, or >.

Examples:

  • <<<<< (Congruent): Center is < → Press Left.
  • <<><< (Incongruent): Center is > → Press Right.
  • XXCXX (Congruent): Center is C → Press Left.
  • XXVXX (Incongruent): Center is V → Press Right.
