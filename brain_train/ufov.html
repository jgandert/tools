UFOV / Speed-of-Processing Task

Train your visual processing speed and field of view. Identify the vehicle in the center and locate the peripheral road sign.

How to Play & Controls

Trial Sequence:

  1. Fixation cross (+) appears. Focus on the center.
  2. Stimulus flashes very briefly (50–300 ms): a vehicle in the center (Car/Truck) and a sign in the periphery.
  3. A noise mask covers the board to prevent retinal persistence.
  4. Prompt 1: Identify the central vehicle.
  5. Prompt 2: Select which of the 8 peripheral locations contained the sign.

Controls:

  • Central Vehicle: Click the Car or Truck button, or press A / ← (Car) or L / → (Truck).
  • Peripheral Location: Click the matching peripheral circle, or press keys 1 to 8 (starting clockwise from top-most circle).

Subtest Modes:

  • Central Processing Only: Only identify the center vehicle (no peripheral signs).
  • Divided Attention: Identify the center vehicle AND locate the peripheral sign.
  • Selective Attention: Center vehicle AND peripheral sign, but other lanes contain circular distractors.
