Dual N-Back Task

Monitor a sequence of visual locations (flashing squares) and auditory letter cues. Press the match keys or buttons when the current stimulus matches the one presented N steps back.

How to Play & Examples

The game presents a sequence of trials. On each trial, a square flashes in a 3x3 grid (Visual) and a letter is spoken (Auditory) at the same time.

N-Back Concept:

  • If N=1: Match with the immediately preceding trial.
  • If N=2: Match with the trial shown two steps ago.

Example (Visual 2-Back):

  1. Trial 1: Top-Left square
  2. Trial 2: Center square
  3. Trial 3: Top-Left square → Match! (Same as Trial 1, two steps back)
  4. Trial 4: Center square → Match! (Same as Trial 2, two steps back)

Key Mappings:

  • Visual Match: Press A or Left Arrow (←)
  • Auditory Match: Press L or Right Arrow (→)

Note: In Visual-only or Auditory-only mode, only monitor and respond to that single modality.
