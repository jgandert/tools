Low-Variance Variant Rules

Adopt these community-tested rules to prevent snowballing and early-game bad luck.

The "Poverty is no Shame" Welfare Token
If the yield number is not a 7, and you receive exactly zero resources from your settlements/cities on that turn, take 1 Welfare Token. Trade 2 Welfare Tokens on your turn to the bank for 1 resource of your choice.
The Friendly Bandit (Edge Case Protection)
When rolling a 7, the Bandit cannot be moved to a hex adjacent to a settlement of a player who currently has the minimum starting victory points. This prevents targeting players stuck at the starting gate.
Blind Bidding Initial Placement
Instead of a snake-draft, players are given a starting budget of 15 Victory Tokens. The first player to place chooses an intersection and bids a number of tokens. Others may outbid. The winner places their settlement and discards those tokens. Repeat until all start. Remaining tokens act as tie-breakers.
Guaranteed First Turn Build
All players begin the game with 1 Brick and 1 Lumber in their hand, in addition to the resources yielded by their second settlement. This dramatically speeds up the notoriously slow first 3 rounds.
